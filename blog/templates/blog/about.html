BVRIT GYM

Equipments and Steps for Usage

1.Dumbbells

1. Hold a dumbbell in both hands and lie on your back with your legs and arms outstretched.
2.Brace your core as you simultaneously lift your right leg and upper body straight up to meet in the air.
3.Touch the dumbbell to your right toe, then lower your upper body and leg with control.
4.Repeat on the opposite leg. That's one repetetion.

15 mins


2.Electric Thread Mill

1.Do a 20 to 30 minute beginner workout.
2.Warm up for 5 minutes.
3.Keep a pace between 3 and 4 mph (4.8 and 6.4 km/h) for 20 minutes.
4.Cool down for 5 minutes, by slowly lowering your pace each minute.

30 mins

3.Outer Inner Thigh

1.Adjust the seat and leg pads to a comfortable position.
2.Press your back firmly against the back rest of the machine.
3.Push against the leg pads of adductor machine with your legs and slowly spread them.
4.Squeeze your adductors and hold for a count of one.

15 mins

4.Bike


1.Adjust the seat and set the handlebars correctly.
2.Get to know the display panel.
3.Adjust the pedal straps so that your feet feel snug — but don’t let the straps cut off your circulation.
4.Don’t pedal with just your toes and Don’t hunch over.

20 mins

5.Leg Press Calf Raise

1.Sit down on a Leg Press Machine and press the plate up as if you were performing a leg press.
2.Slide your feet down so that the balls of your feet are pressing against the rack
3.Keeping the handles locked, press up and flex your toes and then slowly bring your toes back towards your body
4. Perform these movements slowly for maximum benefit.

15 mins

6.Lat Pull Down

1.Keep your chest tall and Keep your back straight
2.Use your lat/back muscles to bring the bar down
3.Squeeze your shoulder blades together and don't Use your biceps too much on the way down
4.Pull the bar down to the top of your chest

8-12 repetetions

7.Smith Machine

1.To perform a Smith machine squat, place the bar from where you’d like to un-rack it, then load the bar.
2.Center the bar and make sure it’s sitting on your shoulders, which are tucked under the bar.
3.Rise back up by pushing the ground down and your shoulders straight up into the bar.
4.You should not feel the bar grinding forward into the Smith machine.

12 repetetions

8.Biceps Curl Bench

1.Set up the bench and Try incline dumbbell curls.
2.Keep your palm inward for a hammer curl.
3.Do concentration curls to help focus movement.
4.Try a Zottman curl by doing the hammer curl in reverse.

9.Cable Cross-over-Machine

1.Stand between two low- to medium-height cables with handles.
2.Squat down, grab each handle, and stand up with your elbows bent and in the starting position for a shoulder press.
3.Step back with one foot so you have more stability. Engage your core and push the cables upward until your arms are extended overhead.
4.Do 2–3 sets of 10–12 repetitions.

4-5 mins
